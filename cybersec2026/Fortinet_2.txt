## Key Points

- **Challenges Facing Modern Enterprises:**
  - Rapid growth of shadow SaaS and Generative AI creates visibility blind spots.
  - Employees frequently use unauthorized applications causing compliance risks and security vulnerabilities.
  - Inconsistent security protection across office, home, and remote environments leads to performance and security issues.
  - Fragmented architectures with multiple products increase operational complexity, overlapping licenses, and escalate total cost of ownership (TCO).
  - Balancing compliance demands with security efficiency and operational simplicity poses ongoing challenges.
  - Future trends bring heightened challenges including hybrid workforce protection, shadow SaaS, generative AI, and BYOD (Bring Your Own Device).

- **Limitations of Current SSE Solutions:**
  - Complex management due to multiple standalone agents and integrations.
  - High licensing costs and many add-on components.
  - Dependency on vendor security effectiveness, limited threat intelligence, SSL inspection performance issues, and concerns over on-premises plus cloud security.

- **Fortinet Unified SASE Solution:**
  - Single management platform with one FortiClient agent integrating antivirus, anti-malware, zero trust, and VPN.
  - AI-driven threat protection via FortiGuard Labs provides real-time automated zero-day threat detection and machine-speed threat hunting.
  - Global footprint of security access points (PoPs) ensures local, consistent security anywhere in the world.
  - VPN transformation to a SASE cloud model improves performance via local inspection and global routing, eliminating bottlenecks and implicit trust.

- **Infrastructure and Architecture Highlights:**
  - FortiSASE global infrastructure backed by $1 billion+ investment, with over 100 cloud locations worldwide.
  - Unified management and data lake to seamlessly fuse networking and security.
  - Supports agent and agentless remote users, SD-WAN (3rd party or FortiGate), and thin edge hardware agents.
  - Specialized FortiAPs and FortiExtender appliances provide security for small, temporary sites and IoT/OT devices.
  - Private and sovereign SASE deployments offer enterprises full ownership, data sovereignty, and regulatory compliance.

- **Security Innovations:**
  - FortiSASE Secure Browser Extension provides seamless, lightweight security for unmanaged or contractor devices with zero disruption.
  - Detailed remote user digital experience monitoring (DEM) offers proactive visibility into latency, jitter, and packet loss to accelerate troubleshooting.
  - Comprehensive generative AI security controls, enabling granular actions like Allow, Monitor, Block, and Warning with continuous FortiGuard Labs AI category updates.
  - BYOD use challenges and solutions including conditional access and secure browser for native experiences.

- **Market Recognition:**
  - Fortinet recognized as a leader in Gartner's SASE platforms (2025).
  - Awarded Gartner Peer Insights Customers’ Choice for SD-WAN and SSE multiple years consecutively.

## Important Conclusions and Insights

- Unified SASE platforms that integrate networking and security into a single, manageable architecture reduce operational complexity, cost, and security risks.
- AI-driven automated threat intelligence and protection are critical to address evolving threats including zero-day attacks and generative AI risks.
- Localized security enforcement combined with a globally distributed infrastructure improves user experience and performance for hybrid and remote workforces.
- Security for IoT and OT requires specialized hardware proxies (FortiExtender) to bridge passive devices into zero-trust architectures.
- Sovereign and private SASE deployments help enterprises comply with stricter global regulations by maintaining data sovereignty and infrastructure control.
- Lightweight, native browser extensions for secure access improve compliance and user adoption compared to deploying new enterprise browsers.
- Active digital experience monitoring (DEM) tools empower IT teams to diagnose user experience issues rapidly across complex environments.
- Fortinet's leadership and customer validation underscore the maturity and effectiveness of its unified SASE solutions.

## Technical and Other Problems Addressed in the Presentation

- **Shadow SaaS and Generative AI Visibility Blind Spots:**  
  Unmanaged personal or unauthorized cloud applications cause security blind spots and compliance issues.

- **Security Inconsistencies Across Environments:**  
  Users work from office, home, and remote locations with uneven security policies, lacking comprehensive visibility and control.

- **Fragmented Security Architectures:**  
  Multiple independent products increase operational overhead, complexity, and redundant licensing costs, raising overall TCO.

- **Existing SSE Limitations:**  
  - Fragmented management with multiple agents.  
  - High licensing costs and add-ons.  
  - Inadequate threat intelligence and limited development.  
  - SSL inspection performance bottlenecks.  
  - Security risks due to hybrid on-prem + cloud deployments.

- **BYOD Risks:**  
  Personal devices may hold sensitive corporate data, and their loss or theft poses data leakage risks.

- **OT/IoT Protection Gaps:**  
  Legacy, closed, or resource-limited devices can’t run modern endpoint protection, leading to high exposure risk and operational challenges.

- **Regulatory Compliance Requirements:**  
  Stricter data sovereignty and privacy laws force the need for private or sovereign SASE solutions with local data control and compliance.

- **User Experience and Adoption Barriers:**  
  Enterprise browsers can cause user disruption, training overhead, and productivity loss; native extensions offer a better alternative.

- **Performance Bottlenecks in Traditional VPNs:**  
  Inefficient backhauling causes latency and implicit trust problems in legacy VPN architectures.
