# Summary of "DIGI Gold Card: Explore Taiwan Opportunities"

### Authors/Presenters
- Kevin Sharma  
  Specialist, Taipei Computer Association  
  Taiwan DIGI Gold Card Community, Administration for Digital Industries  

---

### Key Points

#### 1. Geographic and Transport Advantages
- Taiwan serves as a hub in Asia with easy access to major cities in the Philippines, Japan, South Korea, Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia, Singapore, and Hong Kong within a four-hour flight.

#### 2. Convenience & Affordability
- Efficient public transport (timely MRTs, fast High-Speed Rail).
- Affordable and accessible transportation including Uber, taxis, and YouBike.
- Friendly and tax-free shopping environment, no tipping required, optional self-checkout.
- Convenience stores offer multiple services such as paying bills, shipping, health checking, ticket purchasing, and even government-related transactions.
- Receipt lottery incentivizes spending.

#### 3. Safety & Health
- Taiwan ensures care for residents and their families with good healthcare facilities and a safe living environment.

#### 4. Cost of Living and Quality of Life
- Taiwan's cost of living is significantly lower compared to the US and other major cities.
- Monthly rent, basic meals, public transport, doctor's visits, and crime rates are dramatically more affordable and safer in Taiwan.
- XIAO LONG BAO index (number of dumplings you can buy for $10) highlights affordability.

#### 5. Taiwan DIGI Gold Card Overview
- Special Invitation to international digital-economy professionals to engage and work in Taiwan.
- Covers 12 professional fields including Architecture, Biotechnology, Culture & Arts, Digital, Economy, Education, Environmental Protection, Finance, Law, National Defense, Science & Technology, and Athletics.

#### 6. Employment Gold Card Benefits
- Open work permit with no employer restrictions.
- Family inclusivity: spouse and children can apply for resident visas and work.
- Access to National Health Insurance.
- Income tax reduction on salary exceeding NT$3 million (~US$95,000) for the first 5 years.
- Eligibility for Permanent Residency (PR) after stipulated time based on education level.
- Eligibility for the new pension system.

#### 7. Digital Field Details
- Emphasis on cutting-edge tech skills including AI, IoT, 5G, cloud computing, machine learning, deep learning, image recognition, semantic analysis.
- Digital economy roles such as digital marketing, AR/VR design, fintech, e-commerce, blockchain, and open-source software.

#### 8. Qualification Criteria for Applicants
- Must meet at least one criterion:
  - Professional competence with 8+ years experience in the digital economy.
  - Salary in relevant field exceeding NT$160,000 (approx. US$5,000) monthly.
  - PhD degree from top 500 worldwide universities or relevant expertise with awards and experience.
- Authority reserves the right to review eligibility.

#### 9. Application and Review Process
- The process takes 2 to 4 months depending on the category and document needs.
- Steps include online application, qualification review, passport inspection (for overseas applicants), secondary review, and card issuance.

#### 10. Application Fees
- Fees vary depending on passport status and duration (1-year, 2-years, or 3-years validity).
- Special lower fees for residents in Hong Kong or Macau.

#### 11. Contact and Additional Resources
- Application and LinkedIn QR codes provided.
- Email contact: hello@digigoldcard.tw

---

### Important Conclusions and Insights
- Taiwan is positioning itself as an attractive hub for digital economy professionals by offering a comprehensive and flexible work visa system through the DIGI Gold Card.
- The program encourages talent mobility by simplifying employment and residency requirements, providing family benefits, health coverage, tax incentives, and a path to permanent residency.
- Taiwan’s low cost of living combined with its technological infrastructure offers a high quality of life for expatriates.
- The focus on the digital economy and advanced technologies indicates Taiwan’s commitment to becoming a leading center for innovation and digital talent in Asia.
- The visa and application process is transparent but thorough, ensuring quality and fit of professionals entering Taiwan.

---

### Technical or Other Problems Emphasized in the Presentation
- The presentation does not explicitly emphasize major technical or operational problems but implicitly suggests challenges related to:
  - Verifying and reviewing eligibility for the Gold Card given the high standards of qualifications.
  - Ensuring timely processing within 2-4 months despite diverse background factors and varying document requirements.
  - Integrating international digital professionals into Taiwan’s ecosystem in a seamless manner.
- No direct mention of challenges regarding the digital economy itself or Taiwan’s infrastructure limitations.

---

This summary outlines a comprehensive introduction to the Taiwan DIGI Gold Card program targeted at attracting high-skilled international digital economy professionals through a well-structured visa and support system.
